About me

Hello there! My primary alias online is 56dev_.

I chose it as my username because 56 matches my initials.

I live in Canada (Alberta, oilll babyy!!!!) and I'm a Christian. I first immigrated here at a very young age. I have been online-schooled ever since the pandemic.

I have enjoyed programming (specifically, making progress on programming, which is rare) ever since I was introduced to it at around twelve years of age. My first dive into programming was gamedev with C#. For many years, due to many factors, progress was regretfully slow. Currently, I am learning C++ and it is shaping up to be one of my favorite languages!

Sometimes, I enjoy art; my main inspiration for art, or even just the art I enjoy looking at most, has, for the past year or two, been Tsukumizu's art, as well as similar styles that mimic the cute artstyle and its homey, unique je ne sais quoi, although lately I've been starting to branch out to more diverse styles of art.

I love eggs for breakfast! I've had them practically every day of my life! Don't believe anyone who says it isn't healthy, my body weight is in a healthy range.

I have a little bit more ambition and delusional faith than I rightfully deserve, but I have faith that this faith will pay off. Unconvinced? Well, I have the faith that this faith in this faith will pay off. And then I have the faith in this faith in this faith ...
